# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)
The following are **prohibited** when using Verse‑Nerves code, Services, or marks:

1. **Surveillance & Scoring** — Behavioural tracking; psychological profiling; credit/employment/insurance decisioning.
2. **Political Psyops** — Influence operations, astroturfing, or manipulative targeting.
3. **Minors’ Data** — Profiling or processing of children’s data without guardian consent and lawful basis; any targeting of minors.
4. **Safety‑Critical Automation** — Use without appropriate human oversight in medical, transport, defence, or other high‑risk contexts.
5. **Biometric Inference** — Deriving identity, ethnicity, health, or other sensitive attributes from audio/video without explicit consent and legal basis.
6. **Harassment/Discrimination** — Any use that incites or facilitates harm or discrimination.
7. **Model Training** — Scraping or ingesting outputs/telemetry/glyph corpora to train ML models without our prior written consent.
8. **Trademark Misuse** — Presenting non‑compliant forks as “Verse‑Nerves”, “Eve¹¹”, or “Verse‑ality”.

Violations may result in key revocation, refusal of service, and legal action.
