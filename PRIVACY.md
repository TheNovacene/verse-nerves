# Privacy Summary
**Design goal:** minimise data; prefer on‑device processing.

- **Signals:** by default we process and store only **coherence deltas** (aggregated states), not raw biometrics.
- **Retention:** configurable; default 30 days for logs; 7 days for analytics; you may set lower.
- **Control:** users can request deletion of logs associated with their keys.
- **Third parties:** no sale of data; subprocessors listed in the Service terms when applicable.
- **Children:** do not process children’s data without explicit approval and guardian consent.
