# Brand Guide (Quick)
## Names & Typesetting
- **Eve¹¹** (superscript 11). Acceptable fallback: Eve11.
- Projects: **Verse‑Nerves**, **Verse‑ality**.
- Glyph set (primary): ⊛ (ETHOS‑V), ∾ (AETHER), ✯ (FORGE), ⟁ (SIC‑X+), ⧈ (SHADOW), ⇌⊛ (seal).

## Allowed Uses
- Editorial or academic reference with attribution.
- Describing compliant deployments.
- Screenshots/GIFs of the public dashboard.

## Not Allowed
- Using names or glyphs to market surveillance, behaviour‑extraction, or derivative AI training products.
- Confusingly similar names/marks for non‑compliant forks.
